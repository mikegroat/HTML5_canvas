Here are the things I've been playing with:

Particles!

This generates a bunch of particles and moves them around the full screen canvas.

Connected Particles!

This generages a bunch of particles with connective lines and moves the particles/lines around the full screen canvas.

Bouncing Particles!

This creates a bunch of particles that bounce off one another and the borders of the screen. This version has velocity leaders as well!

Sizzling Particles!

This generages a bunch of particles around the mouse pointer. Click the mouse and see what happens!

Growing Rectangles!

This resembles a multiple frequency volume meter.

Combining Growing Rectangles with Sizzling Particles!

This puts together the growing rectangles and sizzling pointer to see how you can combine these libraries to create interesting effects. Click the mouse and see what happens!

Simple Ball Breaker Game!

Fire balls at rectangles to break them. Get prizes when you balls hit prize markers.
